Privacy & Policy


We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect
a. Personal Information: We may collect personal information that you provide to us when you use our website, such as your name, email address, and phone number, via any forms embedded on the site.
b. Usage Information: We may collect usage information when you use our website, such as your browser type, and device information.
c. Cookies: We might use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your use of our website in future, we aren't using any cookies yet.

2. How We Use Your Information
a. We may use your personal information to respond to your inquiries, and provide all sort of support.
b. We may use your usage information to monitor and improve the performance of our website, analyze user behavior, and prevent fraud.

3. Data Security
a. We implement reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, and use.
b. We use encryption and google's service only to secure your personal information during transmission.
c. We regularly monitor our systems for security vulnerabilities and promptly address any identified vulnerabilities.

4. Data Retention
a. We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.

5. Your Rights
a. You have the right to access and correct your personal information, by contacting us.
b. You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information (which includes only basic info that you might have provided in past).

6. Changes to this Policy
a. We may update or modify this privacy policy at any time without prior notice.
b. Your continued use of our website after any changes indicates your acceptance of the modified privacy policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please Contact Us.
